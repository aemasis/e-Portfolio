Bibliography 04

Textile History

A subject guide I created for my LIS 702 course on facilitating user experience. This webpage is meant to act as an interactive subject guide that provides users with eight different books and four different websites to start exploring textile and material history with. The books range from pop history to academic editions in order to give users different levels of information. The websites link to sites with robust digital collections and even more resources for users to explore.
