Artifact 10

Experimental Film Digital Curation Project

A presentation I did for my digital curation course. The project was centered around curating born digital objects across a wide variety of formats. The organizing theme of my curatorial project was experimental film. I collected a variety of short films, interviews, images, and text documents that related to experimental films and filmmakers. The video found here is a walk through of the curatorial process and objects.

Artifact 12

The Whole Sky On A Page

A website I built for my History of the Book course. The website is thematized around a mock exhibition on the history of astronomy told from the perspective of material book culture. The books range from a Late Babylonian (1000–500 BC) tablet to a first edition of Copernicus' De revolutionibus orbium coelestium from 1543.
